# Shipping Rules Specification Document

## Company Shipping Policy - E-Commerce Platform

### Carrier Selection Rules

**FedEx Rules:**
FedEx is our preferred carrier for medium-weight packages traveling moderate distances. When a package weighs 5 pounds or less and needs to travel up to 100 miles, we use FedEx standard shipping. FedEx offers reliable service with good tracking for packages under 10 pounds going express.

**UPS Rules:**
For heavier shipments over 10 pounds traveling long distances (more than 200 miles), UPS provides better rates and handling. UPS is especially cost-effective for bulk shipments and packages requiring special handling.

**USPS Rules:**
United States Postal Service is ideal for lightweight items. Any package weighing 2 pounds or less traveling under 50 miles should use USPS for the most economical shipping option. USPS First Class is perfect for documents and small parcels.

### Priority and Express Shipping

**High-Value Orders:**
Orders with a total value exceeding $500 automatically qualify for free priority shipping upgrade. This is part of our customer loyalty program to encourage larger purchases.

**Urgent Shipments:**
When customers mark their order as urgent, priority shipping should be enabled regardless of order value to meet delivery expectations.

### Customer Discount Rules

**Senior Citizens:**
Customers aged 65 or older receive a 15% discount on all purchases as part of our senior appreciation program.

**Regional Promotions:**
Customers over 18 years old located in New York receive a 10% promotional discount.

**Volume Discount:**
Orders totaling $500 or more qualify for a 5% bulk purchase discount.

### Sample Formatted Rules

weight <= 5 && distance <= 100 -> CalculateFedExShipping(4.5, 80)
weight > 10 && distance > 200 -> CalculateUPSShipping(12, 250)
weight <= 2 && distance <= 50 -> CalculateUSPSShipping(1.5, 40)
order_value > 500 -> SetPriorityShipping()
age > 65 -> ApplyDiscount(15)
age > 18 && location == "NY" -> ApplyDiscount(10)
total >= 500 -> ApplyDiscount(5)

### Additional Policies

- International shipments require customs documentation
- Hazardous materials need special carrier approval
- Perishable goods must use expedited shipping
- Oversized packages incur additional handling fees

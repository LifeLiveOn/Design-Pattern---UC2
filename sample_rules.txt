# Sample Business Rules Document

## E-Commerce Business Rules

### Discount Rules for Retail Customers

**Senior Discount:**
If a customer's age is greater than 65 years, apply a 15% discount to their order.

**Premium Customer Rules:**
When a customer's age is greater than 18 and their location is New York, apply a 10% discount.

**High-Value Order Rules:**
If the total order amount is greater than or equal to $500, apply a 5% discount to encourage bulk purchases.

### Shipping Charge Calculation Rules

**FedEx Shipping Rules:**
- For packages weighing 5 pounds or less traveling up to 100 miles, calculate FedEx shipping using standard rates
- For express orders with packages under 10 pounds, use FedEx priority shipping

**UPS Shipping Rules:**
- For packages over 10 pounds traveling more than 200 miles, calculate UPS shipping charges
- UPS is preferred for heavy packages due to better rates

**USPS Shipping Rules:**
- For lightweight packages (2 pounds or less) traveling short distances (under 50 miles), use USPS for most economical shipping
- USPS First Class is ideal for documents and small items

**Priority Shipping:**
- Orders with total value exceeding $500 qualify for free priority shipping upgrade
- Urgent orders (marked as urgent) should use priority shipping regardless of value

### Additional Business Logic
- International orders may require additional customs documentation
- Oversized packages need special handling
- Perishable items require expedited shipping
